<p><strong>The Voltage Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Band-pass filter</li><li>Band Rejection Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage filter market encompasses various types of filters designed to manipulate signal frequencies. Low-pass filters allow signals below a specific frequency to pass while attenuating higher frequencies. High-pass filters do the opposite, permitting only signals above a designated frequency. Band-pass filters enable a specific range of frequencies to pass, blocking those outside this range. Conversely, band rejection filters, or notch filters, eliminate a specific frequency while allowing others to pass through, catering to different electronic and communication applications.</p></p>
