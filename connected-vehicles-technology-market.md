<p><p>Connected vehicle technology encompasses various communication types that enhance road safety and traffic efficiency. V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) allows cars to communicate with each other, sharing data about speed, location, and hazards. V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) enables vehicles to interact with road infrastructure, such as traffic lights and signals, improving traffic flow. V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian) focuses on interactions between vehicles and pedestrians, enhancing safety through alerts and guidance. Together, these technologies promote smarter transportation systems and contribute to improved road safety.</p></p>
